Endings

TURKEY 1 and TURKEY 2 thought of calling it quits after what had happened. But they refused to let Roblox win, to strip them of their spirit. The TURKEYS were reborn — this time, as Stickmen. STICKMAN 1, donning his nation’s hat, became the proud blue navy figure you see on the left. Alongside him stood the sky-blue STICKMAN 2, ready to carry on their journey. Together, they spread their message across servers: the TURKEYS you once knew have risen again, reborn as STICKMEN. Their legend grew as they conquered new games — Horrific Housing, Touch Football, Death Penalty, Dead Rails — while also revisiting old favorites to rekindle lost memories. Among these adventures, another stood out: a game called Plz Donate. Why they ventured there remains a mystery… but the answer lies ahead. Stay tuned for the next chapter, arriving Fall 2026.
