Beginnings

21 December 2019 — upon consultation with "stealthninjamaster10" (Stealth) and "AstroElite" (EliteTheMagnificent), an account was created which would go on to become the greatest Roblox account ever made, in just a few years’ time.

Combining Stealth’s "10", Elite’s "Astro", and my own awesomeness, that day gave birth to the infamous and glorified "ASTROAWESOMENESS10."

The three of us would go on to conquer an array of games: Hide and Seek Extreme, Natural Disaster Survival, Theme Park Tycoon, Epic Minigames — and the list goes on.

Many acquaintances were made along the way, but a few were lost. Particularly, EliteTheMagnificent. He sadly departed after becoming an anime merchant, and is remembered to this day — for he was the chosen one, destined to destroy the anime, not join them; to bring balance to Roblox, not leave it in darkness.

ASTROAWESOMENESS10 continued his journey alongside Stealth, and a new companion — Celestia0201, a member of ASTRO’s private domicile. The legendary trio was gone, but two lethal duos had now been forged: Stealth and ASTRO, Celestia and ASTRO.

All was well, as ASTRO conquered game after game — but little did he know, there was far more destined for him.
